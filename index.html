About Us

Driving the Future of Energy AI

We are committed to providing professional evaluation tools and standards for foundation model development in energy management scenarios. Through our open-source benchmark framework, we aim to promote collaboration between academia and industry, accelerating innovation in Home Energy Management Systems and Battery Energy Storage applications.

Our project is inspired by excellent power industry benchmarks like ElecBench, while incorporating the latest trends in foundation models and agentic AI technologies, providing more professional and comprehensive evaluation solutions for home energy management and battery energy storage scenarios.

Join us in building a smarter, more efficient, and more sustainable energy future!
